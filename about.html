What is Hygro?

Sustainable. Wasteless. Grown Efishent.

Mission Statement

Our missions is to bring transparency back into food production; get the consumer back involved in the food they eat; and provide safe, nutritious food. The technology now exists to produce in a safe and sustainable way for the consumers, the workers, and the environment. The farmer is no longer some distant acquaintance, but rather a prominent face in the community they serve.

The goal of our operation is to produce fresh, organic, local food. Our system is designed to meet the nutritional needs of a community - providing both vegetables and protein - that is located within a few miles of the consumer. We will be raising fish and vegetables in a responsible and sustainable manner. Aquaponics is the combination of two existing and established industries: aquaculture and hydroponics.
